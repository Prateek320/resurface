How to use Resurface

Everything you can do on the platform

Quick start

  1. Go to Add Opportunity and paste text from WhatsApp, LinkedIn, a job post, or email.
  2. Click Extract Opportunity with AI (or press Ctrl+Enter).
  3. Review and edit the preview, then Save Opportunity.
  4. Open any card to view details, generate follow-up drafts, and set reminders.

Dashboard

  • Overview toggle: Switch between Summary stat cards and Charts visualizations.
  • Stats: Total active opportunities, high priority (8+), due within 14 days, and followed up.
  • Search: Find opportunities by title, company, tags, or description.
  • Filters: All, High Priority, Due Soon, New, or In Progress.
  • Sort: By priority score, deadline, or newest first.
  • List rows: Click a row for full details. Use Start / Done to track progress.

Opportunity list

  • Priority score: Shown on the left. AI ranks urgency from 1 to 10.
  • Next Action: The specific follow-up step the AI recommends.
  • Archive: Move completed or paused items out of the active dashboard.
  • Delete: Permanently remove an opportunity.
  • View (eye icon): Open the full detail panel.

Detail view & follow-ups

  • Generate Draft: AI writes a ready-to-send email, LinkedIn DM, or message.
  • Copy / Gmail / LinkedIn: One-click actions from the draft panel.
  • Notes: Add your own context; saved automatically.
  • Reminders: Browser notifications for 1 day before, day of, or if stalled 3 days.
  • Snooze: Hide an opportunity for 3 or 7 days.
  • Outcome: Track Applied, Got reply, Rejected, or Ghosted when you mark Done.
  • Activity log: See status changes, drafts, and reminders over time.

Profile & AI personalization

  • Complete onboarding with your role, skills, location, and interests.
  • AI uses your profile to personalize priority scores and follow-up actions.
  • Edit anytime via Profile in the sidebar (when signed in).

Cloud sync & sign-in

  • Sign in with email magic link or Google to sync across devices.
  • Sync uploads local opportunities to the cloud on first sign-in.
  • Without sign-in, data stays in your browser only.
  • Free tier (signed in): 10 AI extractions and 3 drafts per month.

Export & import

  • Export: Download all opportunities as JSON.
  • CSV: Spreadsheet export for titles, deadlines, status, and more.
  • Import: Restore from a previously exported JSON file.

Capture from anywhere

  • PWA: Install Resurface from your browser for an app-like experience.
  • Share target: On mobile, share text from other apps directly into Resurface.
  • Chrome extension: Save selected text or LinkedIn pages (see the extension/ folder).

Tips

  • Hover any button for 3 seconds to see a tooltip explaining what it does.
  • Duplicate detection warns you before saving a similar opportunity.
  • Use Archive to keep your dashboard focused on what needs action now.
